About Us

Building the future of music through authentic artistry and long-term artist development.

Triton On The Radar was founded in 2026 with a clear vision: to discover and develop exceptional musical talent without compromise. We are an independent record label that prioritizes authentic artistic expression over commercial trends.

Our Approach

We believe that great music comes from artists who have the freedom to explore their creative vision. Our role is to provide the resources, expertise, and support needed to help artists realize their potential while maintaining complete creative control.

From production and marketing to distribution and career strategy, we offer comprehensive support tailored to each artist's unique needs and goals. We're not interested in quick wins—we're building careers that last.

Who We Are

Based in San Diego and connected to the UCSD community, we're a team of music industry professionals who are passionate about discovering new talent and helping artists navigate the evolving landscape of the music business.

We operate independently, which means we can make decisions based solely on artistic merit and long-term potential, not quarterly earnings reports or corporate mandates.
