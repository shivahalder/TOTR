FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

HOW LONG DOES IT TAKE TO HEAR BACK ABOUT MY SUBMISSION?

We review submissions weekly and typically respond within 3-5 business days. If you don't hear back within a week, feel free to follow up.

DO I NEED TO BE A UCSD STUDENT TO SUBMIT?

While we prioritize UCSD-affiliated artists, we're open to all San Diego area talent. Campus connection gives priority but talent always comes first.

CAN I SUBMIT UNFINISHED DEMOS?

We only accept finished, mixed tracks. No rough demos or unfinished work. Quality and completion are key.

WHAT GENRES DO YOU ACCEPT?

We focus on Cali Rap, Alt Hip-Hop, R&B, and adjacent sounds. If it has that West Coast vibe, we want to hear it.
